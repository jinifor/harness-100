# Deliverability Optimization

Use this skill to keep newsletters out of spam and in the inbox.

## Core Guidance

- Avoid spam trigger wording in the subject line and body.
- Keep the HTML simple and the link count reasonable.
- Make unsubscribe and sender information clear.
- Check rendering on Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, and mobile.
- Verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC when sending at scale.

## Practical Checks

- Subject line looks human, not spammy.
- Preheader is present.
- All links work.
- Mobile layout is readable.
- Legal elements are included.
