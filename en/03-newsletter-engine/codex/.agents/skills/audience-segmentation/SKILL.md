# Audience Segmentation

Use this skill to choose who gets what in a newsletter.

## Core Guidance

- Segment by behavior, engagement, attributes, and recency/frequency.
- Match content depth to reader intent and familiarity.
- Create separate strategies for new, active, at-risk, and dormant readers.
- Use segmentation to raise open rate, click rate, and retention.

## Practical Models

- BEAR: Behavior, Engagement, Attribute, Recency-Frequency
- Welcome series for new readers
- Re-engagement sequence for at-risk readers
- Send-time optimization by audience type
