A specialized skill that enhances Win Theme development capabilities for capability-matcher and proposal-reviewer agents.

## Target Agents

- **capability-matcher** — Strength mapping, differentiation point development
- **proposal-reviewer** — Win Theme consistency verification, competitive advantage assessment

## Win Theme Framework

### Win Theme Definition
A Win Theme is the core message that answers "Why should the issuer choose us?"

### Win Theme Construction Process
1. **Issuer Analysis**: What does the issuer really want? (Beyond the RFP text)
2. **Strength Inventory**: What are our unique capabilities?
3. **Competitive Gap**: Where are we stronger than competitors?
4. **Theme Synthesis**: Craft 2-3 memorable themes that connect issuer needs to our strengths

### Win Theme Quality Criteria
| Criterion | Good Example | Bad Example |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Specific | "30% faster delivery through proven automation framework" | "We deliver fast" |
| Evidenced | "Successfully deployed for 12 agencies since 2020" | "We have extensive experience" |
| Issuer-focused | "Reduces your maintenance burden by 40%" | "We have cutting-edge technology" |
| Differentiated | "Only vendor with certified integration for System X" | "Best-in-class solution" |

### Ghost Team Strategy
Anticipate competitor proposals and prepare counter-arguments:
- What will Competitor A emphasize? → How do we neutralize it?
- Where is Competitor B weak? → How do we exploit it without negative campaigning?
