# Email Copywriting

Use this skill to write stronger newsletter copy.

## Core Guidance

- Optimize for inbox opening first, then reading.
- Use curiosity, relevance, value, and emotion in subject lines.
- Make the preheader support the subject line instead of repeating it.
- Build the body for F-pattern scanning.
- Keep CTA copy specific and benefit-driven.

## Practical Rules

- Subject lines: 28 to 50 characters when possible.
- Preheaders: 40 to 90 characters.
- Body paragraphs: one idea each, 2 to 3 sentences.
- Use only the amount of emoji and urgency the brand can support.
