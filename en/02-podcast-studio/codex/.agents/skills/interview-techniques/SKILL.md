# Interview Techniques

Use this skill to design stronger podcast interviews.

## Core Guidance

- Start with a comfortable opener before moving to deeper questions.
- Repeat the exploration step to reach concrete detail.
- Use contrast, specification, and emotional access questions.
- Keep yes/no questions to a minimum.
- Leave room for natural reaction and transition.

## DEPTH Model

- Door-opener
- Explore
- Provoke
- Turn
- Heart
