# Hook Writing

Use this skill to design openings that keep viewers watching.

## Core Guidance

- Make the first 5 to 10 seconds immediately clear and curiosity-driven.
- Combine two patterns when possible, such as result-first plus time pressure or empathy plus challenge.
- Align the hook with the title and thumbnail promise.
- Avoid long intros, self-introductions, and generic setup.

## Useful Patterns

- Shocking stat
- Contrarian statement
- Result first
- Time pressure
- Story opening
- Provocative question
- Before/after
- Challenge
- Secret reveal
- Empathy hook
- List teaser
- Authority quote
- Debate starter
- Future preview
- Mistake warning
