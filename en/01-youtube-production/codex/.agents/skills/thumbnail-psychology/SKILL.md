# Thumbnail Psychology

Use this skill to improve thumbnail click-through potential.

## Core Guidance

- Make the topic obvious in 3 seconds or less.
- Use a simple composition with one focal point.
- Keep text short, large, and mobile-readable.
- Choose colors that create contrast with competing thumbnails.
- Use emotional triggers such as curiosity, surprise, tension, or achievement.

## Quick Checks

- Does the thumbnail match the video promise?
- Is the overlay text complementary to the title?
- Can the thumbnail be understood on a small mobile screen?
- Does the color palette separate the thumbnail from competitors?
